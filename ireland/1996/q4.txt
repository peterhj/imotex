Let $F$ be the midpoint of side $BC$ of triangle $ABC$.

Construct isosceles right triangles $ABD$ and $ACE$ externally on sides $AB$ and $AC$ with the right angles at $D$ and $E$, respectively.

Show that $DEF$ is an isosceles right triangle.
