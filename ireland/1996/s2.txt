It is clear that $S(a+b) \leq S(a) + S(b)$, with equality if and only if there are no carries in the addition of $a$ and $b$. Therefore $S(2n) \leq 2S(n)$. Similarly $S(2n) \leq 5S(10n) = 5S(n)$.

An example with $S(n) = 1996S(3n)$ is $133\cdots35$ (with 5968 threes).
