The diagonals of a trapezoid $ABCD$ intersect at point $P$.
Point $Q$ lies between the parallel lines $BC$ and $AD$ such that
  $\angle AQD = \angle CQB$, and line $CD$ separates points $P$ and $Q$.
Prove that $\angle BQP = \angle DAQ$.
