A convex quadrilateral $ABCD$ satisfies $AB \cdot CD = BC \cdot DA$.
A point $X$ is chosen inside the quadrilateral so that $\angle XAB = \angle XCD$
  and $\angle XBC = \angle XDA$.
Prove that $\angle AXB + \angle CXD = 180^\circ$.
