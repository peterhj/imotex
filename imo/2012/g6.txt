Let $ABC$ be a triangle with circumcenter $O$ and incenter $I$.
The points $D$, $E$, and $F$ on the sides $BC$, $CA$, and $AB$ respectively are
  such that $BD + BF = CA$ and $CD + CE = AB$.
The circumcircles of the triangles $BFD$ and $CDE$ intersect at $P \neq D$.
Prove that $OP = OI$.
