Point $P$ lies inside triangle $ABC$.
Lines $AP$, $BP$, $CP$, meet the circumcircle of $ABC$ again at points $K$, $L$,
  $M$, respectively.
The tangent to the circumcircle at $C$ meets line $AB$ at $S$.
Prove that $SC = SP$ if and only if $MK = ML$.
