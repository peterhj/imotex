Let $ABCDE$ be a convex pentagon such that $BC \| AE$, $AB = BC + AE$, and
  $\angle ABC = \angle CDE$.
Let $M$ be the midpoint of $CE$, and let $O$ be the circumcenter of triangle
  $BCD$.
Given that $\angle DMO = 90^\circ$, prove that $2 \angle BDA = \angle CDE$.
