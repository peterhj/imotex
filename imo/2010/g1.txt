Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with $D$, $E$, $F$ the feet of the altitudes
  lying on $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ respectively.
One of the intersection points of the line $EF$ and the circumcircle is $P$.
The lines $BP$ and $DF$ meet at point $Q$.
Prove that $AP = AQ$.
