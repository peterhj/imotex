The vertices $X$, $Y$, $Z$ of an equilateral triangle $XYZ$ lie respectively on
  the sides $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ of a triangle $ABC$.
Prove that if the incenter of triangle $ABC$ lies outside triangle $XYZ$, then
  one of the angles of triangle $ABC$ is greater than $120^\circ$.
