Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $AB = AC \neq BC$ and let $I$ be its incentre.
The line $BI$ meets $AC$ at $D$, and the line through $D$ perpendicular to $AC$
  meets $AI$ at $E$.
Prove that the reflection of $I$ in $AC$ lies on the circumcircle of triangle
  $BDE$.
