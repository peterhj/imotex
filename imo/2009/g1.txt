Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $AB = AC$.
The angle bisectors of $A$ and $B$ meet the sides $BC$ and $AC$ in $D$ and $E$,
  respectively.
Let $K$ be the incenter of triangle $ADC$.
Suppose that $\angle BEK = 45^\circ$.
Find all possible values of $\angle BAC$.
