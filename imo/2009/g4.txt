Given a cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$, let the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ meet at $E$
  and the lines $AD$ and $BC$ meet at $F$.
The midpoints of $AB$ and $CD$ are $G$ and $H$, respectively.
Show that $EF$ is tangent at $E$ to the circle through the points $E$, $G$, and
  $H$.
