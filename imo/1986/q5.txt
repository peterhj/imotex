Find all functions $f$, defined on the non-negative real numbers and taking
  non-negative real values, such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $f(xf(y))f(y) = f(x+y)$ for all $x$, $y \ge 0$,
\item $f(2) = 0$,
\item $f(x) \neq 0$ for $0 \le x < 2$.
\end{enumerate}
